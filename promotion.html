Different ways to promote:

Create a Social Media Presence

Optimizing on social media directs traffic towards your website if the accounts are linked. A lot of people use social media, so this is a good source for promotion.

Link Popularity

Find a way to get listed on other sites! The more credible sites that link to Lanwell Publishing, the more traffic comes in - and, the better we rank.

General Advertising

Ever the old standby, advertising is a reliable way to gain more traffic. Buy ads on other sites, or do a billboard, etc. Sometimes, an ad can be more efficient in reaching the user than a simple link. And, you'd possibly be reaching out on social media, which doesn't require the user to be actively searching for you.
